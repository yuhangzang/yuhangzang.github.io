Yuhang Zang

Hi, I am Yuhang Zang (臧宇航), a 4-th year Ph.D. student at MMLab@NTU, Nanyang Technological University, supervised by Prof. Chen Change Loy. Prior to that, I received my Bachelor degree from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2019.

My current interests involve perception algorithms (e.g., detection, segmentation) under real-world constraints (e.g., open-set recognition, long-tailed distribution, multi-modality representation).
