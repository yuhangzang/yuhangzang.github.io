About Me

I am a 3rd year Ph.D. student at MMLab@NTU, Nanyang Technological University, supervised by Prof. Chen Change Loy.

Prior to that, I received my Bachelor degree from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2019.

My research interests are object detection and instance segmentation algorithms under real-world constrains (e.g, open-world data splitting, long-tailed distribution, multi-modality representation).
